Task 1
List all countries and customers related to those countries.
Display country name in English, city name and customer name.
Return even countries without cities and customers.

SELECT country.country_name_eng,
city.city_name,
customer.customer_name
FROM country
LEFT JOIN city
ON country.id = city.country_id
LEFT JOIN customer
ON city.id = customer.city_id;

Task 2
Return all countries that have cities.
For those cities return all customers.
Return even cities that do not have customers.

SELECT country.country_name_eng,
city.city_name,
customer.customer_name
FROM country
INNER JOIN city
ON country.id = city.country_id
LEFT JOIN customer
ON city.id = customer.city_id;
